# entry-points

The composition detail pane's entry-point editor: the roots the closure is
resolved from, as removable chips plus an add-search over every known plugin id.

Entry points are the composition's *seed*, not a preference — everything the
Summary counts and the Closure section tints is reachable from this list. An
empty entry-point list resolves to an empty bundle, which is why a new
composition looks inert until one is added.

`allIds` (the searchable candidate set) comes from `useCompositionData()` — the
full registry, not the current closure, since the point of the control is to
reach plugins the draft does not yet include.
